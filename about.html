Our Journey

Founded with a vision to preserve and promote Bharatanatyam as a living tradition, Renga Nrithyalaya has been at the forefront of classical dance education since its inception. What started as a small classroom has now grown into a premier institution nurturing hundreds of students in the divine art form of Bharatanatyam.

Guru Aksharha Gopinath

Director
Mrs. Aksharha Gopinath holds the position of Executive Lead in Govt Agenexy and serves as the Director of Renga Nrithyalaya School, school was named by her Spiritual Guru Maharanyam Sri Sri Muralidhara Swamigal. She received her Bharatanatyam training from Guru Dr. Srividya Sundaresan, a dedicated instructor in the art form. Beginning her training at the age of 6, she completed her Arangetram at 13. Simultaneously pursuing a diploma in Bharatanatyam from Bharathidasan University alongside her Engineering studies in IT, she demonstrated a strong commitment to both disciplines. While employed at esteemed companies such as Cognizant and Paypal, she also imparted dance lessons to students in Chennai, India. Furthermore, she received early music training from her mother, Mrs. Sulochana Gopinath, and further honed her skills under the guidance of Mrs. Bhuvaneshwari Chandrasekar, who belongs to the Thiyagaraja Shishya Parampara.
